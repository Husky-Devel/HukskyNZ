Butter Chicken

Ingredients

1 large chicken breast

50g butter

½ onion chopped

½ t crushed garlic

2 T tomato paste

1 carrot finely diced

¼  cup peas

½ t each of Nutmeg, cumin,ginger,Ground chilli,turmeric and garam masala

Salt and pepper to taste

200ml cream

Method

Cut chicken into even sized chunks

Mix spices into a bowl

Add chicken and mix until all the chicken is coated with the spices

In a frypan, melt butter, add onion and garlic and finely diced carrot

Add chicken and cook until chicken is cooked

Add tomato paste and mix well

Slowly add cream mix well.

Add peas

Simmer for about 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked.

Serve with rice
