Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

2 C cooked rice

2 T oil

1 chicken breasts, diced into small pieces

2 eggs

1 onion finely chopped

1 t crushed ginger

1 carrot finely sliced

½ cup corn

½ c peas

2 - 3 T soya sauce

Method

In a frying pan add 1T oil and beaten eggs.

Mix until cooked, remove from the pan and set aside.

Heat a frying pan with 1T oil, add the onions , carrots and ginger and saute until soft.

Add the diced chicken and cook through.

Add the peas and corn stir and heat through.

Add cooked rice and stir well.

Add soya sauce and mix.

Lastly add cooked egg and mix.
